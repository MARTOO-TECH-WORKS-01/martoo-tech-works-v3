Download FAQs

Common questions about our software downloads.

Why are downloads password protected?

We password-protect our downloads to ensure they are only accessed by legitimate students and researchers. This helps us maintain the integrity of the software and comply with licensing requirements.

What is the password?

Each software has its own unique password displayed on its download card. Passwords are case-sensitive and follow the format: MARTOOTECH@SOFTWARE (e.g., MARTOOTECH@SPSS, MARTOOTECH@STATA, MARTOOTECH@QGIS).

How do I download?

Click the "Download" button, enter the password when prompted, and you'll be redirected to Google Drive. Click the download icon on Google Drive to save the file to your computer.

Are these files safe?

Yes. All downloads are scanned for malware and verified before being made available. We've been providing these downloads to Zambian students since 2022 with zero security incidents.

How do I extract the files?

You'll need WinRAR, 7-Zip, or built-in Windows extraction tools. Right-click the downloaded file, select "Extract All," and enter the password when prompted.

Need help with installation?

Contact our support team via WhatsApp or email, and we'll guide you through the installation process. We offer free installation support for students.

Need Installation Assistance?

Our team is ready to help you with installation, configuration, or any technical issues.
