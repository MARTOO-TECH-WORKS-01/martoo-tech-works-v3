Introduction

Martoo Tech Works ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

We are a Zambian company operating in compliance with Zambian data protection laws and international standards including GDPR where applicable. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Our Commitment

We take your privacy seriously. All client data is treated with strict confidentiality, and we never sell your personal information to third parties. Our systems are designed with privacy as a core principle.

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our website and services, including:

Personal Information You Provide

When you register, enroll in courses, request services, or contact us, we may collect:

Data Type Examples Purpose
Identity Data Name, username, date of birth Account creation, personalization
Contact Data Email address, phone number, physical address Communication, service delivery
Academic Data Institution, course of study, research interests Service customization, course recommendations
Payment Data Payment method, transaction details (NOT credit card numbers) Billing and invoicing
Service Data Project details, research data, analysis requests Service delivery and support

Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns, including:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, links clicked, referring website
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device (see our Cookie section below)

Important Note on Research Data

Any research data, theses, or academic work you share with us remains your intellectual property. We do not claim ownership over your work and will not use it for any purpose without your explicit consent.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To Provide and Maintain Services: Process enrollments, deliver courses, complete service requests
  • To Communicate With You: Respond to inquiries, send administrative information, provide technical support
  • To Improve Our Platform: Analyze usage patterns, troubleshoot issues, enhance user experience
  • To Personalize Your Experience: Recommend courses and services relevant to your interests
  • To Process Payments: Bill for services and manage subscriptions
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: Maintain records, respond to lawful requests

No Selling of Personal Data

We do not and will never sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is yours alone.

Legal Basis for Processing

Our legal basis for collecting and using your personal information depends on the specific context and the type of information processed:

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our service obligations to you (e.g., course delivery, data analysis)
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services, prevent fraud, and secure our platform
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional data collection (you may withdraw consent at any time)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with Zambian laws and regulations

Information Sharing

We may share your information with the following categories of third parties:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us operate our business (payment processors, cloud hosting, email services). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Professional Advisors: Lawyers, auditors, and consultants who provide professional services to us.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

We do not share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology.
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication requirements and role-based access to personal data.
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing.
  • Employee Training: All team members receive training on data protection and privacy.

Security is Our Priority

While no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, we follow industry best practices to protect your information. We use Vercel and Firebase, which maintain ISO 27001 certification and SOC 2 compliance.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for 2 years thereafter
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 5 years to comply with tax and financial regulations
  • Service Data: Retained for the duration of the project and 1 year after completion, unless otherwise agreed
  • Communications: Retained for 2 years

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in the "Contact Us" section. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and understand where our visitors come from.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for website functionality (login, shopping cart) Session / Persistent
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our site 2 years

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our website.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of Zambia. We use service providers (such as Vercel and Firebase) that may store data in multiple jurisdictions. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will notify you by email (if you have an account) or through a prominent notice on our website prior to the change becoming effective.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for the latest information about our privacy practices.
