Bridging Public Health & Digital Innovation

Martoo Tech Works is a Zambian digital solutions provider dedicated to empowering researchers, public health professionals, and students with cutting-edge GIS mapping, STATA analysis, and academic support services.

Our Story

Founded in Lusaka, Zambia, Martoo Tech Works emerged from a clear observation: public health professionals and students across Zambia lacked access to affordable, high-quality technical support for their research and academic work.

What began as freelance consulting for university students quickly grew into a full-service digital solutions platform. Today, we've supported over 100 researchers, trained hundreds of students, and partnered with leading health organizations to advance public health outcomes through data and technology.

Our journey is driven by a simple belief: that the right tools and guidance can transform Zambian research and public health practice.

"We don't just provide services – we build capacity. Every project, every training, every map we create is an investment in Zambia's future public health leaders."

2022 Founded in Lusaka
