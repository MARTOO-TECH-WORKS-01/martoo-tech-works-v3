Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our services and support.

How quickly do you respond?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. Urgent requests marked with the priority checkbox are prioritized and typically receive a response within 4-6 hours.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer a 15-minute free consultation to discuss your project needs and provide a customized quote with no obligation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money (Airtel, MTN), bank transfers, and cash. Payment plans are available for larger projects upon request.

Do you work with clients outside Lusaka?

Absolutely! We work with clients across all provinces of Zambia via WhatsApp, email, and virtual meetings. We also offer on-site support in selected areas.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we treat all client data with strict confidentiality. We sign NDAs upon request and never share your information or project details with third parties.

Do I need a formal agreement?

For projects above K500, we provide a formal service agreement outlining scope, deliverables, timeline, and payment terms.
