Introduction

At Martoo Tech Works, we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with our services, courses, and products. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds are issued. We believe in fair and transparent practices that respect both our clients and our business sustainability.

This policy applies to all payments made to Martoo Tech Works, including:

  • Academy Courses: Single course purchases and subscription plans
  • Professional Services: GIS mapping, STATA analysis, research support, academic services
  • Software Downloads: Password-protected software packages
  • Consulting Services: One-on-one consultations and training sessions

Our Promise

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, we'll work with you to make it right. Our goal is fair resolution, not hiding behind fine print.

Refund Eligibility

Refund eligibility varies depending on the type of product or service purchased. The table below summarizes our refund policy for each category:

Product/Service Refund Period Eligibility Fee
Single Course Purchase 7 days Eligible No fee
Course Subscriptions 14 days Prorated No fee
Professional Services Before work begins 100% refund No fee
Services in Progress N/A Partial (work completed) 15% admin fee
Completed Projects N/A Not eligible N/A
Software Downloads N/A Not eligible N/A
Consulting Sessions 24 hours before Eligible No fee
Duplicate Payments 30 days 100% refund No fee

Important Note

All refund requests must be submitted within the specified timeframes. Requests submitted after the refund period will not be eligible for a refund.

Academy Courses

Single Course Purchases

You may request a full refund within 7 days of purchase if you have not completed more than 30% of the course content. Refunds after 7 days or after completing more than 30% of the course are not available.

Monthly Subscriptions

You may cancel your monthly subscription at any time. Refunds for the current billing period are provided on a prorated basis for unused time. To receive a prorated refund, you must cancel within 14 days of your billing date.

Annual Subscriptions

Annual subscriptions may be refunded on a prorated basis for the unused portion of the subscription year, minus a 15% administrative fee. Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date.

Free Courses

Free courses are not eligible for refunds as no payment was collected. Access to free courses may be revoked at any time at our discretion.

Professional Services

Our professional services include GIS mapping, STATA data analysis, research proposal writing, academic support, and consulting. Refund eligibility depends on the stage of the project:

Before Work Begins

100% Refund

If you cancel before any work has commenced on your project, you are eligible for a full 100% refund of any amounts paid.

Work in Progress

Partial Refund

If you cancel while work is in progress, you will receive a refund for the portion of work not yet completed, minus a 15% administrative fee. You will receive all work completed up to the cancellation date.

Project Complete

No Refund

Once a project has been completed and delivered to you, no refunds are available. However, we offer 14 days of free revisions to ensure your satisfaction with the delivered work.

Satisfaction Guarantee

For all completed service projects, we provide up to 14 days of free revisions. If you are unsatisfied with the delivered work, please contact us immediately to discuss revisions. We are committed to delivering work that meets your requirements.

Software Downloads

Password-Protected Software

Software downloads, including SPSS 16, STATA 14, and QGIS, are provided as password-protected files. Due to the digital nature of these products and the immediate access upon download, all software download sales are final and non-refundable.

Exception: Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues that prevent you from installing or using the downloaded software, please contact our support team. We will work with you to resolve the issue. If we are unable to resolve the issue within 7 days, a full refund will be issued.

Subscriptions

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.

Prorated Refunds

Prorated refunds for unused portions of subscriptions are available under the following conditions:

  • Monthly subscriptions: Refund requests within 14 days of billing date
  • Annual subscriptions: Refund requests within 30 days of purchase date
  • All prorated refunds are calculated based on the number of unused days remaining in the billing period

Failed Payments

If a subscription payment fails, we will attempt to process the payment up to 3 times over 5 days. If all attempts fail, your subscription will be suspended. No refunds are issued for failed payments that are later successfully processed.

Refund Process

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Email refunds@martootech.com or use our contact form with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Provide Information: Include your full name, email address used for purchase, date of purchase, amount paid, and reason for refund request
  3. Attach Receipt: If available, attach a copy of your payment receipt or transaction confirmation
  4. Submit: Send your request and await confirmation from our team

Required Information

To process your refund efficiently, please include the following information in your request:

  • Full name and email address
  • Date of purchase
  • Product or service purchased
  • Amount paid
  • Transaction ID or receipt number (if available)
  • Reason for refund request

Email Your Refund Request

Send all refund requests to:

refunds@martootech.com

Processing Timeline

Once your refund request is received and approved, refunds are processed according to the following timeline:

  • Acknowledgment: Within 24 hours of receiving your request
  • Review Period: 2-3 business days to verify eligibility and process the request
  • Payment Processing: 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account (varies by payment method and financial institution)

Total estimated time: 7-14 business days from request submission to refund receipt.

Expedited Processing

For verified duplicate payments and clear billing errors, we prioritize processing within 48 hours.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are strictly non-refundable:

  • Software Downloads: All password-protected software files (SPSS, STATA, QGIS)
  • Completed Service Projects: Projects that have been delivered and accepted by the client
  • Free Courses: No payment was collected, therefore no refund is applicable
  • Donations: Any voluntary payments or contributions made to Martoo Tech Works
  • Third-Party Fees: Payment processing fees charged by banks or payment providers (these are non-recoverable)

Duplicate Payments

If you accidentally make a duplicate payment, we will issue a full refund for the duplicate transaction(s). Please contact us with the following information:

  • Your name and email address
  • Date and amount of the duplicate payment
  • Transaction IDs for both payments (if available)

Duplicate payment refunds are processed within 48 hours of verification and do not incur any administrative fees.

Cancellations

Course Cancellations

You may cancel your enrollment in a paid course within 7 days of purchase for a full refund, provided you have not completed more than 30% of the course content.

Subscription Cancellations

Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time through your account dashboard. Cancelled subscriptions remain active until the end of the current billing period. No further payments will be charged after cancellation.

Service Cancellations

To cancel a professional service project, please contact us immediately. Refunds will be processed according to the timeline outlined in the Professional Services section.

Payment Disputes

If you believe a charge is incorrect or unauthorized, please contact us immediately before filing a dispute with your bank or payment provider. We are committed to resolving all billing issues directly and promptly.

Frivolous Disputes

Filing a chargeback or payment dispute without first attempting to resolve the issue with us may result in permanent suspension of your account and access to all purchased services and courses. We are reasonable people – please talk to us first.
